About Me

I am Jaehee Kim, a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at POSTECH, supervised by Prof. Youngjoo Lee. Currently, I am also a visiting researcher at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, working with Prof. Zhengya Zhang.

My research interests lie in the digital hardware design for Forward Error Correction (FEC) and Quantum Error Correction (QEC) systems.
